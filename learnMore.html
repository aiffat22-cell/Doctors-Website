    Dental Care

    Dental care involves the maintenance of oral health, including the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of various dental conditions. It includes regular check-ups, cleanings, and treatments such as fillings, extractions, and root canals.

    Medical Checkup

    Medical checkups are comprehensive examinations performed by healthcare professionals to assess an individual's overall health and detect any potential medical issues. These checkups often involve physical examinations, medical history review, and may include blood tests and diagnostic screenings.

    Physical Therapy

    Physical therapy is a healthcare specialty that focuses on improving and restoring physical function and mobility. It involves exercises, stretches, and other therapeutic techniques to help individuals recover from injuries, manage chronic conditions, and improve their overall physical well-being.

    Surgery

    Surgery is a medical procedure that involves invasive techniques to treat or correct various health conditions. It may be conducted for diagnostic purposes, to remove or repair diseased tissues, or to alleviate symptoms. Surgeries can range from minimally invasive procedures to complex operations requiring significant recovery time.
